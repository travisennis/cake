**Source:** prompt.md item 6

**Priority:** P2

**Effort:** M

**Problem:** `ResolvedModelConfig { config: ModelConfig, api_key: String }` leads to awkward access such as `config.config.model` and does not strongly distinguish resolved credentials.

**Files involved:** `src/config/model.rs`, backend modules, `src/clients/agent.rs`
