# Revisit macOS Sandbox Probe Caching and Cleanup

**Source:** prompt.md items 50 and 51

**Priority:** P3

**Effort:** M

**Problem:** `probe_can_apply_profile` caches capability for the process lifetime and creates scratch files under the temp directory. Probe failures can produce misleading diagnostics, and temp files may accumulate.

**Files involved:** `src/clients/tools/sandbox/macos.rs`, `src/clients/tools/bash.rs`

**Fix:** Use `tempfile` cleanup semantics, improve probe error reporting, and document whether process-lifetime caching is acceptable.
